I am a postdoc at Harvard University, working with Prof. Stratos Idreos on self-designing data and AI systems. Before that, I received my PhD from Université Paris Cité with Prof. Themis Palpanas, and my master's and undergrad degrees from Fudan University with Prof. Wei Wang and Prof. Peng Wang. I am also fortunate to closely collaborate with Prof. Ke Li at Simon Fraser University.
